Background of BBLC

Our journey from a small initiative to a comprehensive educational center

Our Founding Story

Boys Brigade Learning Centre (BBLC) was established in 2010 by a group of passionate educators and community leaders who recognized the critical need for quality educational opportunities for children in underserved communities of Phnom Penh.

What began as informal English classes for just 20 students has grown into a comprehensive educational program serving hundreds of students annually. Our founders believed that education is the most powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty and creating lasting change in communities.

Initially operating from a small rented space with limited resources, BBLC steadily expanded its programs and outreach through the dedication of volunteers and the support of donors who believed in our mission.

Educational Impact

Our students show significant improvement in academic performance, with many going on to pursue higher education opportunities that would otherwise be inaccessible to them.

Community Development

BBLC has become a community hub, offering not just education but also support services, parental guidance, and a safe space for children to learn and grow.

Holistic Approach

Beyond academics, we focus on character development, life skills, and creative expression to help students become well-rounded individuals prepared for future challenges.

Our Journey

2010

Foundation

BBLC was established with just 20 students and 3 volunteer teachers offering basic English classes in a small rented space.

2012

First Permanent Location

Moved to our first dedicated learning center, allowing us to expand our programs and serve more students.

2013

First Computer Lab

We opened our first computer lab with 10 donated computers, beginning our digital literacy program.

2015

Curriculum Expansion

Introduced life skills and character development programs to complement our academic offerings.

2016

Music Program Launch

Introduced guitar and music classes to foster creativity and self-expression among our students.

2018

Community Outreach

Expanded our services to include community support programs and parental education workshops.

2020

COVID-19 Response

Adapted our programs to include remote learning and distributed educational materials to students' homes during pandemic closures.

2022

Technology Upgrade

Completely upgraded our computer lab and introduced coding classes for advanced students.

2023

Expansion

Opened a second learning center to serve more communities in need across Phnom Penh.

Looking to the Future

As we look ahead, BBLC remains committed to our mission of empowering Cambodian youth through education. Our future plans include expanding our programs to reach more communities, developing vocational training for older students, and strengthening our alumni network to create lasting impact.

We believe that by investing in education today, we are building a brighter future for Cambodia—one student at a time.
