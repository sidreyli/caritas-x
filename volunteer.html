Why Volunteer with Us?

Volunteering at Boys Brigade Learning Centre is more than just giving your timeit's about being part of a transformative experience that changes lives, including your own. Our volunteers play a crucial role in empowering students to break the cycle of poverty through education.

Whether you're a teacher, professional, student, or simply someone passionate about making a difference, there are many ways to contribute your skills and time to our mission.

Volunteer Opportunities

English Teachers

Lead English classes for students of all levels, from beginners to advanced. Help students develop communication skills that will open doors to better opportunities.

  • Native or fluent English speaker
  • Teaching experience preferred but not required
  • Patience and enthusiasm for working with students
  • Cultural sensitivity and adaptability
Time Commitment: Minimum 3 months, 2-4 hours per week

Computer Skills Instructor

Teach basic computer literacy, Microsoft Office, and digital skills. Help students develop technological competency essential for modern careers.

  • Proficiency with computers and software
  • Ability to explain technical concepts clearly
  • Experience with educational technology preferred
  • Patience with different learning speeds
Time Commitment: Minimum 2 months, 3-5 hours per week

Administrative Support

Assist with program coordination, student records, volunteer management, and general administrative tasks that keep our center running smoothly.

  • Strong organizational skills
  • Basic computer proficiency
  • Attention to detail
  • Good communication skills
Time Commitment: Flexible, 2-6 hours per week

Mentorship Program

Provide one-on-one guidance and support to students, helping them with academic goals, career planning, and personal development.

  • Life experience and wisdom to share
  • Good listening and communication skills
  • Commitment to building relationships
  • Cultural awareness and respect
Time Commitment: Minimum 6 months, 2-3 hours per week

Media & Communications

Help document our programs, create content for our website and social media, and assist with fundraising materials and communications.

  • Photography or videography skills
  • Social media experience
  • Creative writing abilities
  • Basic design skills (preferred)
Time Commitment: Flexible, project-based

Creative Arts Instructor

Teach music, art, drama, or other creative subjects. Help students express themselves and develop confidence through artistic activities.

  • Skill in music, art, or performing arts
  • Ability to work with groups
  • Creativity in lesson planning
  • Enthusiasm for the arts
Time Commitment: Minimum 2 months, 2-4 hours per week

The Volunteer Experience

Our volunteers consistently tell us that their time at BBLC has been one of the most rewarding experiences of their lives. You'll work alongside dedicated local staff and form meaningful connections with students who are eager to learn and grow.

We provide comprehensive orientation and ongoing support to ensure you feel confident and prepared. You'll receive training on our teaching methods, cultural context, and how to work effectively with our student population.

Many volunteers extend their stay or return for additional terms because of the strong bonds they form and the visible impact they see in their students' progress.

How to Apply

Our application process is designed to ensure a good match between volunteers and our needs, setting everyone up for a successful and meaningful experience.

Submit Application

Complete our online application form with your background, skills, interests, and availability. Include any relevant experience and motivation for volunteering.

Interview

Participate in a virtual or in-person interview where we'll discuss your goals, our programs, and how we can work together effectively.

Background Check

Complete a background check and provide references. This helps us ensure the safety and security of our students and volunteers.

Orientation

Attend our comprehensive orientation session covering our programs, cultural context, teaching methods, and expectations.

Start Volunteering

Begin your volunteer assignment with ongoing support and regular check-ins to ensure a positive experience for everyone.
