Guitar Classes

Learn to play guitar while developing creativity, self-expression, and cognitive skills

Join Our Classes Support Music Education

Music Education Programme

Our guitar programme introduces students to the wonderful world of music through hands-on learning and creative expression. Music education has been proven to enhance cognitive development, improve academic performance, and build confidence in young learners.

From complete beginners to those with some musical experience, our classes provide a supportive environment where students can discover their musical talents and develop lifelong skills.

What Students Learn

Basic Guitar Skills

Students start with fundamental guitar techniques including proper posture, finger positioning, and basic chord progressions.

  • Guitar anatomy and care
  • Basic chord formations
  • Simple strumming patterns
  • Reading chord charts

Music Theory

We introduce age-appropriate music theory concepts that help students understand the structure and language of music.

  • Note recognition
  • Basic rhythm patterns
  • Song structure understanding
  • Musical expression

Performance Skills

Students build confidence through regular practice sessions and opportunities to perform for their peers and families.

  • Solo performance
  • Group playing
  • Stage presence
  • Musical collaboration

Benefits of Learning Guitar

Cognitive Development

Learning guitar stimulates multiple areas of the brain simultaneously, improving memory, concentration, and problem-solving skills. Students often see improvements in their academic performance as well.

Music education helps develop pattern recognition, mathematical thinking, and language skills - all while having fun and expressing creativity.

The discipline required to learn an instrument also teaches patience, perseverance, and goal-setting skills that benefit students in all areas of life.
