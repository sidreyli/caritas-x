Your Impact

See Where Your Money Goes

We believe in complete transparency. Every dollar you donate is carefully tracked and reported, so you can see exactly how your contribution is making a difference in our students' lives.

Your support doesn't just provide educationit offers hope, opportunity, and the chance for a better future. Many of our graduates have gone on to pursue higher education and secure better employment because of the foundation they built here.

Join our community of supporters who are committed to breaking the cycle of poverty through education.

$25 provides

School supplies for one student for a full month, including notebooks, pens, and educational materials.

$50 provides

Nutritious meals for one student for an entire month, ensuring they can focus on learning without hunger.

$100 provides

Complete educational support for one student for one semester, including all materials and resources.

$500 provides

Computer access and digital literacy training for an entire class, preparing students for modern careers.
