Mission, Vision & Values

Guiding principles that shape our work and impact in Cambodia

Our Mission

To empower Cambodian youth through quality education, life skills training, and character development, enabling them to break the cycle of poverty and become positive change-makers in their communities.

Our Vision

A Cambodia where every child has access to quality education and opportunities to realize their full potential, contributing to a prosperous and equitable society.

Our Values

These core values guide everything we do at Boys Brigade Learning Centre:

  • Empowerment through education
  • Compassion and service
  • Integrity and accountability
  • Community partnership
  • Innovation and adaptability

Our History

Founded in 2010, Boys Brigade Learning Centre began as a small initiative to provide supplementary education to children in underserved communities in Phnom Penh. What started as informal English classes taught by volunteers has grown into a comprehensive educational program serving hundreds of students annually.

Over the years, we've expanded our offerings to include computer literacy, music education, and life skills training, always responding to the evolving needs of the communities we serve.

2010

Foundation

BBLC was established with just 20 students and 3 volunteer teachers offering basic English classes.

2013

First Computer Lab

We opened our first computer lab with 10 donated computers, beginning our digital literacy program.

2016

Music Program Launch

Introduced guitar and music classes to foster creativity and self-expression among our students.

2020

COVID-19 Response

Adapted our programs to include remote learning and distributed educational materials to students' homes.

2023

Expansion

Opened a second learning center to serve more communities in need.
