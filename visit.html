Welcome to BBLC

We warmly welcome visitors who want to see our educational programs in action, meet our dedicated teachers, and interact with our enthusiastic students. Visiting our centre gives you a firsthand look at the transformative power of education in our community.

Whether you're interested in volunteering, making a donation, or simply learning more about our mission, a visit to our centre is an inspiring experience that showcases the real impact of your support.

What to Expect During Your Visit

During your visit, you'll have the opportunity to observe classes in session, meet our dedicated teachers, and interact with our enthusiastic students. You'll see firsthand how our programs are making a real difference in their lives.

We'll provide you with a guided tour of our facilities, including classrooms, computer lab, and common areas. You'll also learn about our different programs and how they're designed to meet the unique needs of each student.

Many visitors leave feeling inspired by the positive energy and determination of our students, and often become long-term supporters of our mission.
