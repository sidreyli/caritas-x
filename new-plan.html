Strategic Plan 2025-2030

Building the future of education in Cambodia through innovative programs and sustainable growth

Our Strategic Pillars

Educational Excellence

Enhance curriculum quality and teaching methodologies to improve learning outcomes for all students.

  • Implement modern teaching technologies
  • Develop specialized skill-based programs
  • Strengthen teacher training initiatives
  • Expand multilingual education options

Community Impact

Strengthen partnerships with local communities to expand our reach and maximize positive impact.

  • Establish satellite learning centers
  • Partner with local schools and organizations
  • Develop community volunteer programs
  • Create family engagement initiatives

Sustainable Growth

Build a financially sustainable organization that can continue to serve communities for generations.

  • Diversify funding sources
  • Develop social enterprise initiatives
  • Build strategic partnerships
  • Strengthen organizational capacity

Innovation & Technology

Embrace innovative approaches and technology to enhance educational delivery and accessibility.

  • Integrate digital learning platforms
  • Develop mobile education solutions
  • Create online resource libraries
  • Implement data-driven decision making

5-Year Implementation Timeline

2025

Foundation Year

Launch new curriculum frameworks, establish teacher training programs, and begin community outreach initiatives.

2026

Expansion Phase

Open first satellite learning center, implement technology platforms, and launch vocational skills training programs.

2027

Innovation Integration

Deploy mobile learning solutions, establish community partnerships, and develop sustainable funding models.

2028

Scale & Impact

Launch second satellite center, establish social enterprise initiatives, and expand family engagement programs.

2029

Optimization

Refine programs based on impact data, strengthen organizational capacity, and prepare for next strategic cycle.

2030

Sustainable Future

Achieve financial sustainability, complete comprehensive impact assessment, and develop next 5-year strategic plan.
