Our Team

Meet the dedicated individuals who make our mission possible

Leadership Team

Sophal Chen

Founder & Director

Sophal founded BBLC in 2010 with a vision to provide educational opportunities to underserved children in Cambodia. With over 15 years of experience in education and community development.

Ratha Mey

Education Coordinator

Ratha oversees our educational programs and ensures we deliver high-quality instruction to all our students. She holds a Master's in Education from the Royal University of Phnom Penh.

Vannak Sim

Program Director

Vannak develops and implements our various educational initiatives and community outreach programs. He is passionate about creating sustainable change through education.

Teaching Team

Sreyneath Lim

English Teacher

Sreyneath has been teaching English at BBLC for 5 years. She specializes in making learning fun and engaging for students of all ages.

Chantha Sok

Computer Instructor

Chantha brings his IT expertise to our computer classes, teaching students essential digital skills for the modern world.

Dara Kong

Music Teacher

Dara leads our guitar and music program, sharing his passion for music with our students and helping them discover their creative talents.

Leakhena Yim

Assistant Teacher

Leakhena supports our main instructors and provides individualized attention to students who need extra help with their studies.

Volunteer Coordinators

Kimleng Tan

Volunteer Manager

Kimleng coordinates our volunteer program, matching skills with needs to ensure both volunteers and students have a rewarding experience.

Samnang Hou

Community Liaison

Samnang builds relationships with local communities and helps identify students who would benefit most from our programs.

Join Our Team

We're always looking for passionate individuals who want to make a difference in the lives of Cambodian youth. Whether you're interested in teaching, administration, or volunteering, we'd love to hear from you.

At BBLC, you'll join a dedicated team working together to create educational opportunities and transform communities through the power of learning.
