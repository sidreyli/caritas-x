About BBLC

Empowering Cambodian youth through quality education and holistic development

Who We Are

Boys Brigade Learning Centre (BBLC) is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing quality education and holistic development opportunities for underprivileged children and youth in Cambodia. Since our founding in 2010, we have been committed to empowering the next generation through education, character building, and skills development.

Our centre serves as a beacon of hope for families in underserved communities, offering free educational programs that bridge the gap between traditional schooling and practical life skills needed for success in the modern world.

Our Story

What started as a small initiative to teach English to 20 children has grown into a comprehensive educational program serving hundreds of students each year. Our founders recognized that education is the most powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty.

From humble beginnings in a rented space, BBLC has evolved into a well-established learning center with multiple programs, dedicated teachers, and a strong community of supporters who believe in our mission.

Today, we continue to expand our reach, always staying true to our core values of education, empowerment, and community development.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We approach every student with empathy and understanding, recognizing their unique challenges and potential.

Excellence

We strive for the highest quality in our educational programs and continuously improve our methods and curriculum.

Community

We believe in the power of community and work to strengthen bonds between students, families, and volunteers.

Innovation

We embrace new teaching methods and technologies to provide the most effective learning experiences.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our operations and relationships with students and partners.

Growth

We are committed to the continuous growth and development of our students, staff, and programs.

Our Impact

Over the years, BBLC has transformed the lives of countless students and their families. Our alumni have gone on to pursue higher education, secure better employment opportunities, and become leaders in their communities.

We measure our success not just in numbers, but in the positive changes we see in our students' confidence, skills, and life trajectories. Every graduate represents a family lifted out of poverty and a community strengthened.

Our holistic approach ensures that students don't just learn academic subjects, but also develop character, leadership skills, and a sense of social responsibility.
