Empowering Cambodian Youth Through Education

The Boys Brigade Learning Centre provides educational opportunities and life skills to underprivileged children in Cambodia.

Support Our Mission Visit Our Centre

Welcome to Our Centre

Since our establishment, the Boys Brigade Learning Centre has been dedicated to providing quality education and holistic development programs for children in Cambodia. We believe in empowering the next generation through education, skills training, and character development.

Our programs range from academic support to extracurricular activities, all designed to help children reach their full potential and break the cycle of poverty.
